Twinkle, twinkle, little star,
So radiant and bright you are.
In the sky above so high,
You adorn the velvet night sky.
Like a diamond, you shimmer and gleam,
Illuminating our nightly dream.
Guiding us with your gentle light,
Through the vast expanse of night.
Twinkle, twinkle, little star,
A constant presence from afar.
You bring a twinkle to our eyes,
And fill our hearts with sweet surprise.
As we gaze up at your celestial show,
Wondering what secrets you truly know.
Do you hold the tales of galaxies old?
Of stories yet untold, waiting to unfold?
Twinkle, twinkle, little star,
How wondrous and mysterious you are.
You inspire us to dream and explore,
To reach for the heavens and strive for more.
In your twinkling, we find delight,
A captivating, enchanting sight.
Oh, twinkle on, and never cease,
Spreading your celestial peace.
Twinkle, twinkle, little star,
A reminder of how small we are.
Yet in your light, we find our place,
A part of this cosmic dance we embrace.
So, twinkle on, with your pure light,
A beacon shining through the night.
Twinkle, twinkle, little star,
Forever cherished, no matter where you are.
